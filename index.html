ABOUT US


Welcome to Deccan Cubing, a community helping in developing speedcubing in Hyderabad and South India. We shall share all the details and updates regarding WCA competitions especially in Hyderabad. Tips to develop your skills from Cubing to organising official competitions as well as unofficial competitions in south India!! With the support of our members and all awesome cubers from Hyderabad and across India, we hope that we will make a great show always and make a good mark in India .

WHAT IS SPEEDCUBING?

Previous Next

Speedcubing (also known as speedsolving) is a sport involving solving a variety of combination or twisty puzzles, the most famous being the 3x3x3 puzzle or Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible. The current world record for solving the 3x3 puzzle is 3.47 seconds!

WHO ARE WE?

Previous Next


We are a bunch of Enthusiastic Cubers from Hyderabad with different backgrounds, age groups and professions joined together with a common agenda of promoting speedcubing across Hyderabad and India which led to the formation of Deccan Cubing. So we are creating a dedicated online platform for speedcubing competitions, to develop cubing activities such as Organising, Training, providing support to organisers and promote cube competitions in Hyderabad and India.

HISTORY

  2. April 16th 2017 : Curtain Raiser of deccan cubing logo and launched Deccan cubing officially across social platforms.
  3. July 15th 2017 : Cube meet for the first official competition.
  4. August 13th 2017 : Successively organized first official Competition.
